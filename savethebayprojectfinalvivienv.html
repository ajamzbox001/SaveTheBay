Summary of findings and recommendations

The structure of existing non-for-profit websites mostly have their story in the About Us section and tell about what they do in the Service section. After that, they will allow their community to donate to the organization in the Donate section. For my recommendations, the website should have 4 pages: Home, About Us, Our Services and Donate. Also, it should have the Footer that contains: Contact Information, Social Media Icons and Privacy Policy. Another recommendation is the website should have a clean design that will make it easy for the users to navigate the site information. Also, it needs a simple and standout logo, too.
